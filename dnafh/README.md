# Definitely Not a Fire Hazard

ATTiny micro controller board to drive a WS2812 LED Strip in the capsules.
Includes a 5V boost converter and single cell LiPo charger with protection circuit.

It needs 2 strips of 7 WS2812 LEDs each for the top and bottom. Holes are for the 30 LEDs/m kind.

The bottom offers space for the board and a CR123A LiPi cell.
